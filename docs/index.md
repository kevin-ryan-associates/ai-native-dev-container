# The AI-Native Engineer

A playbook for software engineers on the path to becoming AI-Native programmers.

## What is an AI-Native Engineer?

An AI-Native Engineer is a software developer who deeply integrates artificial intelligence into their daily workflow, not just as a tool, but as a core part of their development philosophy. They architect systems designed for intelligent automation, leverage AI pair programming effectively, and continuously adapt to the rapidly evolving landscape of AI-assisted development.

## Research Topics

Explore the key areas that define the AI-Native engineering discipline:

- **Agents & Models** — Understanding AI coding agents, model selection, and the ecosystem of intelligent tools.
- **Containers & Infrastructure** — The foundation of modern, portable development environments.
- **SDLC** — Spec-driven development, vibe coding, and new approaches to the software lifecycle.
- **Governance & Sovereignty** — Navigating the regulatory and ethical landscape of AI in software engineering.
